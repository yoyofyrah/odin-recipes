How to make a Gin and Tonic

About

Gin and tonic made in a highball glass with ice, lime juice, and a lime wedge. So refreshing!

Ingredients

  • 4 cubes ice
  • 2 fluid ounces gin
  • 4 fluid ounces tonic water
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 lime wedge

Directions

  1. Place ice cubes in a tall, narrow glass. Pour gin over ice; add tonic water and lime juice. Stir well with a long-necked spoon. Garnish with lime wedge.
  2. Drink
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until inebriated.
