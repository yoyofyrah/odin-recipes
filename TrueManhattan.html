How to make a "True" Manhattan

About

Follow this recipe to make the best Manhattan cocktail, then serve it straight up or on the rocks. Most Manhattan drinkers prefer this classic drink to be stirred, not shaken. (Only shake it when asked to do so!)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
  • Â½ fluid ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 fluid ounces whiskey
  • 1 dash bitters
  • 1 maraschino cherry for garnish

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Fill a mixing glass with ice cubes. Pour in vermouth, then whiskey.
  3. Stir, then strain into a cocktail glass.
  4. Add bitters.
  5. Garnish with cherry.

For a Dry Manhattan

Follow the recipe, but use dry vermouth instead of sweet, and lemon peel instead of a cherry.
